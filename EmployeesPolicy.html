Revolution Soft. Pakistan Privacy Policy

Revolution Soft. Pakistan is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our employees, both remote and on-site. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and the rights of our employees regarding their personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from our employees in order to establish, maintain, and manage their employment relationship with the Company. This information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal identification information such as name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Professional information such as job title, department, and job duties
  • Employment and payroll information such as salary, benefits, and tax information
  • Performance evaluations and other employment-related records

We may also collect additional information as needed to comply with legal and regulatory requirements, such as information related to immigration status, work authorization, and background checks.

Use of Personal Information

We use the personal information we collect from our employees for the following purposes:

  • To establish, maintain, and manage their employment relationship with the Company
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To communicate with employees about their employment status and benefits
  • To evaluate employee performance and make decisions about promotions, transfers, and other employment-related matters

Protection of Personal Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information we collect from our employees. These measures include physical, technical, and administrative controls to secure our systems and data.

We also limit access to personal information to those employees who have a business need to access the information. Any employee found to be in violation of our privacy policy will be subject to disciplinary action.

Employee Rights

Our employees have the right to access, correct, and delete the personal information we collect from them. They can also request that we limit the use of their personal information or object to our use of their personal information.

To exercise these rights, employees should contact the Company's Human Resources department.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. We will notify employees of any changes to this Privacy Policy through email or through the Company's internal communication channels.
