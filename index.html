Privacy Policy

At UET LMS, we take the privacy of our users seriously. This privacy policy applies to all users of our web-based learning management system (LMS) app, which is designed for students at the University of Engineering and Technology in Lahore.

Information Collection

Our app features quick access links to quickly access the desired page and an app-level authentication system that requires users to log in with their email and password. We do not share the names or emails of our users with any third-party apps or services.

We may collect certain information about our users, such as their browsing history and preferences, in order to improve the functionality and usability of our app. This information is collected and used solely for the purpose of improving our app and providing the best possible experience for our users.

Information Use

We may also use the information collected to send users important updates and notifications related to their use of the app. We will not use this information for any other purpose or share it with any third parties without the user's explicit consent.

Children's Privacy

UET LMS (app) is intended for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.
